HUGO BOSS's annual report has to live up to the brand — precise, premium and visually distinctive — across a printed PDF and an online experience. The task was to art-direct that report and evolve it year after year within a consistent system: a fresh visual and motion concept around each year's given theme, without ever losing the brand's discipline.

The work began with a pitch. As lead designer, I owned the pitch design and helped shape its guiding theme — and we won it.

For three years I looked after HUGO BOSS and their annual report. Each year I translated the client's given theme into its own visual and motion concept — a direction that carried across the PDF cover, highlight pages, text animations and video, and even guided work produced outside the studio, such as a CEO video by another agency, so the report stayed coherent across every touchpoint.

TBD

Alongside the yearly concept, I reworked an existing highlights dashboard the client was rightly unhappy with, developed new sections for the landing page, and designed a new interactive graphic for a magazine page. I created charts and new icons in the HUGO BOSS style throughout. In 2025 I developed a detailed storyboard with concrete animation directions and references, working closely with the motion department to bring it to life.
