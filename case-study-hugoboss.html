For a global fashion brand like HUGO BOSS, the annual report needs to reflect the same level of quality and precision as every other brand touchpoint. The challenge was to create an experience that felt unmistakably HUGO BOSS while presenting financial and corporate information across both print and digital.

After leading the successful pitch design in 2021, I became Lead Designer for the annual reports from 2023 to 2025. Over these three reporting cycles, I was responsible for evolving the report while translating each year's annual theme into a fresh visual direction.

For each edition, I developed a visual concept that shaped the report across every medium. I designed the annual cover, defined the overall visual direction and created detailed storyboards for the hero video, establishing the foundation for each year's report.

The visual direction extended beyond my own work. Colleagues and external partners followed the same creative direction across the printed report and supporting assets, including the CEO video, resulting in a cohesive brand experience throughout.

Motion

Motion played an important role in the visual identity of the Hugo Boss report.

For the 2025 edition, I developed detailed storyboards and animation concepts, working closely with the motion team from the first idea through final production. The result was a refined and cohesive digital experience, with motion supporting the overall visual direction.

Digital Experience

Alongside the annual visual concept, I continuously refined the digital report experience. I redesigned the highlights dashboard, introduced new landing page sections and created interactive editorial features that encouraged exploration beyond the mandatory reporting content.

Throughout the report, I designed custom charts, icons and visual assets that aligned with the HUGO BOSS design language while making complex information easier to scan and understand.
