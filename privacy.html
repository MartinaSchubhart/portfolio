Privacy Policy

This policy explains how personal data is processed when you visit this website, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Austrian Data Protection Act (DSG). We take your privacy seriously and keep data processing to the technical minimum.

What this site does not do

This website uses no cookies, no analytics or tracking tools, no advertising, and no contact forms. Nothing is stored in your browser (no localStorage, sessionStorage or similar). Fonts are served from our own server, so no data is sent to external font providers.

Server log files (hosting)

When you access this website, the hosting provider automatically collects and stores access data in server log files that your browser transmits: IP address, date and time of the request, the page/file requested, the referring URL, and your browser type, version and operating system. This processing is technically necessary to deliver the website and to ensure its stability and security. This data is not merged with other data and is not used to identify you personally. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functional, secure website). Hosting provider: vercel.com — see their privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-notice.

Content delivery networks (CDNs)

To display animations and smooth scrolling, this website loads two JavaScript libraries from third-party content delivery networks:

• GSAP, delivered via Cloudflare (cdnjs) — Cloudflare, Inc., 101 Townsend Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA (privacy policy).
• Lenis, delivered via jsDelivr — jsDelivr / ProspectOne (privacy policy).

When these files are loaded, your IP address is transmitted to the respective provider, which is technically required to deliver the files to your browser. These providers set no cookies through this integration and no tracking takes place. Data may be processed on servers outside the EU (e.g. the USA) on the basis of the providers' appropriate safeguards (such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses). Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in an appealing, performant website).

External links

This website contains links to external sites (e.g. LinkedIn and referenced projects). These are only called up when you actively click them; we have no influence over how the operators of those sites process data, and their own privacy policies apply.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to the website or the legal situation. Version: 07 2026.
