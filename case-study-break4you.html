Break4You
Online-Coach

The challenge
Client
Austrian Pancreatic Cancer Support Group & selpers
Year
2020
Role
Lead Designer
Scope of work
Concept / Branding / UI/UX / Print Design / Frontend-Development

Break4You is a free online coach for people caring for a relative with advanced cancer — a group under enormous emotional and physical strain who rarely take time for themselves. Developed as a collaboration between the Austrian Pancreatic Cancer Support Group and selpers, the platform needed to provide practical support while feeling approachable, reassuring and easy to use.

As Lead Designer, I was responsible for shaping the product from the first concept through to launch, combining branding, UX/UI design and visual communication into one cohesive experience.

Concept & Branding

Working closely with the selpers team and members of the support group, I helped develop a six-week programme that gradually unlocks new content, audio guides and exercises. The structure encourages participants to build small moments of self-care into their daily routine without feeling overwhelming.

The visual identity brings both partner organisations together. I developed the logo, colour palette and typography based on the existing brands, making the collaboration visible while giving Break4You its own recognisable identity. To support the educational content, I created a custom illustration style with warm colours and simple forms, helping make the exercises feel approachable and easy to understand.

The Experience

The platform was designed around simplicity and reassurance. From a single dashboard, participants can access weekly content, find consultation hours and quickly navigate to frequently asked questions.

Alongside the product itself, I designed the landing page, onboarding experience and supporting communication materials, including printed information cards distributed through support groups at launch.
